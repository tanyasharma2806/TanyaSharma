My Story

From analyzing HR processes to architecting them.

2019 — 2021

The Analytical Foundation — Deloitte

I began my career as a Business Analyst, setting up HR Shared Services for global clients. This is where I learned to see HR through a systems lens — process gaps, data flows, and scalable design — across healthcare, energy, and technology industries.

2021 — 2023

The People Turning Point — BYJU'S

Moving into a full HRBP role for a 150+ member Sales division, I discovered my passion for the human side of the business — coaching, engagement, and retention. Reducing attrition from 20% to under 5% taught me that culture change is built one relationship at a time.

2023 — Present

Strategic Partnership — L'Oréal India

Today, at L'Oréal's Research & Innovation Division, I combine everything I've learned — analytics, empathy, and business acumen — to be a true strategic partner to leadership, driving talent, learning, and workforce decisions that matter.
